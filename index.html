The Problem

India's biomass supply chain
is completely broken.

Farmers burn what industries desperately need. There is no organised system connecting them — until now.

🔥

Farmers Burn Residue

Paddy straw, wheat stubble and sugarcane trash are burned in open fields — polluting air and wasting income-generating biomass.

Fragmented, Unorganised Supply

No standardisation, no traceability, no quality grading. Aggregators operate in silos with no market visibility or price discovery.

Zero standardisation

Broken Logistics

₹2,400 Cr lost/yr

Cement, power and paper plants face irregular supply, quality variation and no long-term sourcing reliability — raising energy costs.

Irregular supply = high cost
